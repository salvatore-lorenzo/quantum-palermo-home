# Short bio

I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Engineering Physics from Instituto Superior Técnico, Lisbon, in 2023, where for my thesis I worked on Equations of State of Axionic Dark Matter supervised by Prof. Hugo Terças. I then completed my Master’s degree at the same institution, developing my thesis within the q.m@t group under the supervision of Prof. Pedro Ribeiro and Dr. Giuseppe De Tomasi. My work focused on exploring measurement statistics as possible post-selection free probes of measurement-induced phase transitions in monitored free-fermion systems.

I am currently a pre-doctoral researcher at the University of Palermo, where I work with Prof. Federico Roccati on deep thermalization in open quantum systems. My research interests include non-equilibrium quantum physics, quantum information, and thermalization in quantum many-body systems.
